Available throughout The Township of King are Public, Catholic, and Private educational facilities. The Township offers post secondary education at Seneca College King Campus and has also established a substantial rural campus in the Township on the former Eaton estate. The University of Toronto and Humber College are major landowners and may soon be locating here.
